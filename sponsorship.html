📱 What is CampusTrend?

CampusTrend is a student-built social and academic platform designed exclusively for the University of Education, Winneba (UEW). It brings the entire campus experience into one digital space:

  • 📚Past Questions — Exam papers by faculty & level
  • 🎓Tutorials — Student-created learning videos
  • 🛒Campus Market — Buy & sell within the UEW community
  • 📣Social Feed — Share posts, follow classmates, stay on trend

Built with passion, operated on a shoestring budget, and growing every day.
