How to share your video in 3 steps
1
Go to drive.google.com and upload your video file. Any format works — MP4, MOV, AVI, etc.
2
Right-click the video → Share → click Restricted → change to ✓ Anyone with the link → click Done.
This step is critical. If you skip it, viewers will see "You need access" instead of the video.
3
Click Copy link and paste it below ↓ (e.g. https://drive.google.com/file/d/…/view)
Video is not publicly accessible
Your Google Drive file is still set to Restricted. Viewers will see "You need access" instead of the video.

To fix: open the link → Share → change Restricted to Anyone with the link → Done → then paste the link here again.
